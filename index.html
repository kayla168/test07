GorgeoFastener specializes in providing tailored fastening solutions for a wide range of industries. Whether you're looking to enhance the uniqueness of your product or streamline field operations with repeatable processes, custom fasteners can deliver significant value.

To create a custom fastener, three key factors must be considered: the design, the order quantity, and production lead times.

The first step is to submit a detailed CAD drawing or blueprint that outlines the essential specifications of the fastener, such as: Diameter, Thread pitch, Length, Material, Finish.

If you’re unsure about how to design or order a custom fastener, GorgeoFastener is here to assist. With over 10 years of combined experience, we have the expertise to ensure you receive precisely the fasteners you need, delivered on time and within budget.
